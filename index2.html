About Zazonta

Background

The idea springs from another idea that one of the founders got when he was at WHU the German entrepreneurship school. From the start, Zazonta was supposed to be an app for companies to find more and better candidates in a more time and cost effective way.

While investigating the market via internet, we run into some new problems. First we had problems with finding the companies they were searching for, due to the algorithms Google have set up for their search function, which are only beneficial for the companies that put a lot of money into SEO, and have a larger IT department. Another thing we realized was, how time consuming it was to find companies via internet. A problem such as this was not only annoying rather it intrigued us. After a while we became slightly obsessed with the problem. Why is internet so omnipotent yet so complex was a question which kept keeping them up during the night. Without even realize it themselves, their focus started to shift, from a head hunting app into something bigger and more beneficial for the companies. Their focus now laid in the realm of connections and networking online. Well, this might sounds like something you heard before maybe like LinkedIn, but the beauty of this, is that even though, we are frequently at LinkedIn, none of the founders thought of their solution as a direct competitor of LinkedIn. It was not until one of the founder’s grandmother mentioned that they are the LinkedIn for the B2B industry, that they realized that this association might be made in the future, no matter how far off that comparison ever felt for us.

With this not so disruptive paper, we at Zazonta of Sweden, will let you in to our world where we will articulate why our idea is both disruptive yet simple to understand and use. The aim is to give u insight in how we think, both now but also our long-term goals and visions. For us it is important to not only give you facts and number, but also give you a tool to understand us better. Of course, we will present facts, such as market research, studies and other useful information. On top of that, we would like to engage you into some numbers, but the most important number for you to remember is our phone number, because nothing is as powerful as a real life interaction between the founders and the interested second party.

Our Values

MISSION: Our mission is to Blah Blah Blah.....

VISION: The vision is to become the market leader in Europe for business efficiency within the B2B sector. The aim is to support and facilitate the B2B industry from need recognition until after sale. In order to do so we will have to help them to move the business journey online. For a company like ours, we are convinced that a vision is needed. But in order to articulate the company to its fullest extent as well as stay true to our vision, the vision should be broken down and applied to the various parts of the company. Both a futuristic vision, but also a vision for the platform as well as a vision for the employees, in order to keep them as well as keep them motivated and innovative.

Company in the future vision: Zazonta has a vision maybe a bit different from other startup companies. In today’s world people are talking about becoming a Unicorn and fast growth. We at Zazonta have great visions in becoming the leading company for online IT and Marketing/Communications services in Europe within 7 years, but we all stress the importance of growing in a sustainable way. We do think that, much because we would like to keep the company down to earth to be able to challenge the big internet players out there. To become the leading company in Europe, we need to be open for innovations all along the way. We have already started to skis on a few projects that will help us stay innovative, as well as it will help other entrepreneurs to get out of the starting blocks. Making five years plans has proven to be a slippery slope (just look at the Soviet), yet we believe that setting goals and having a vision is essential, in order to stay on track.

Platform vision:"As simple as a child book, yet as sophisticated as handmade watch". This words summarizes the vision we have for the platform. In other words, we need to ensure ease for the users. The narrative is that it should be easy to navigate, understand, hence, making business simple. Yet it has to have sophisticated tools, which undertakes the time and cost losing activates of today. Example of that is a lead generation system which enables the sales department to follow and interact in real time with its leads.

Employee vision: The key to become market leader and stay so, is to never stagnate. In order to not do so, we have a vision of keep encouraging people within and outside the company to be innovative. We believe that fun, openness and opportunities are the key ingredients to encourage this mindset. Along with this we also believe that a we feeling will help people to feel secure, and secure and confident people have the courage to challenge the norm, which is of the highest importance when being innovative.
